## Abstract

This paper introduces **Proof of Growth** — a blockchain consensus mechanism in which verified physical plant cultivation replaces computational work as the source of token value.

Where Bitcoin's Proof of Work asks machines to prove they expended energy through mathematical computation, Proof of Growth asks humans to prove they grew life from the earth. The token produced is called **GMF — Grow My Food**. The protocol is open, decentralized from inception, and belongs to humanity.

No growth — no token. The supply of GMF is directly and permanently tied to the amount of real food grown in the physical world by real human hands.

## The Protocol

**Proof of Growth** is a blockchain consensus mechanism where:

- A player grows real food on real land
- A device called **Grownode** — integrating AI camera, soil sensors, GPS and computer vision — verifies real plant growth across multiple simultaneous data streams
- Verified growth is recorded on-chain as a **Proof of Growth event**
- That event mints **GMF tokens** to the grower's wallet
- No growth event = no new tokens — ever

The protocol runs on a purpose-built blockchain where Proof of Growth is the consensus layer itself. It is not a smart contract on an existing chain. It is the fundamental law of a new chain, fully decentralized from the first block.

---

## The Token — GMF

**GMF (Grow My Food)** is a utility token with real-world proof behind every unit:

- Minted exclusively through verified growth events
- Emission rate determined by crop type, verified green mass, and growth duration
- Fixed maximum supply, encoded at genesis
- Listed on decentralized exchanges via cross-chain bridge
- Community-governed treasury funded by marketplace transaction fees
- Anti-inflation by design: no administrator key, no mint function outside the Proof of Growth pathway

## The First Implementation — 013TRIBE

Proof of Growth is an open protocol. **013TRIBE** is the first application built on it — a physical gardening game where growing food is the act of play.

**Phase 1 — Beta**
Indoor growing box. No land required. City-accessible. First pioneer players grow the first wave of food and earn the very first GMF tokens. Pioneer status carries into Phase 2.

**Phase 2 — Full Launch**
Real land. Outdoor Grownode. Full Proof of Growth verification. Complete token economy and marketplace.

The protocol itself is not owned by 013TRIBE. Anyone can build on it.

## The Female Model of Bitcoin

Bitcoin is outward energy — machines expend electricity to emit computational force. Like the sun.

Proof of Growth is inward energy — humans tend the earth, which receives, transforms, and generates life. Like the earth.

These are not moral categories. They are descriptions of energy direction. The sun and the earth need each other. But until now, only the sun had a blockchain.
